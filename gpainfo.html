Info on GPA calculator

Our GPA calculator solely calculates an unweighted GPA (out of 4.0), though a weighted GPA calculator is in the works. Keep in mind that your school may use a different scale to calculate your GPA, so while the GPA this calculator returns will be accurate for the standard unweighted model, your school may assign you a different figure. I am always looking to improve our site, so if you have any questions, inquiries, or suggestions, please reach out to rmogauro@gmail.com. Thanks!
